Hi! I'm Carolyn

Who

Tenacious, warm, and free-spirited, I am always ready to hit the ground running. I love humming, playing the ukulele, and coming up with new melodies. Also, since living with three guys, I've grown very fond of craft beer, and am always on the hunt for them, preferably stouts. When I'm not coding, you can also find me catching pokemon. Better yet, if you're in the area, we can go pokemon hunting together.

What

Since working through simple coding algorithms in ruby earlier this year, I have unearthed a new hunger to learn more about web development and to pursue it at a professional level. My fascination has led me to explore responsive design and innovative technologies that I now use in my own work. I also enjoy incorporating my love of logic, colors, and clean code to deliver beautiful and well-designed layouts.

Where

Originally from Boston, MA, I currently live in Atlanta, Georgia, after making the decision to attend Digital Crafts, a full stack web developer training program. I am working on further advancing my skillset in web development and am set to graduate from the program in early January 2017. I am also open to relocating for work once the opportunity arises.
